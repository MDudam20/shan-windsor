Location Highlights

Where Progress Comes with A Great View

Located 34 Km. north of Mumbai, Malad is one of the most popular suburbs and residential areas which enjoys the benefits of location and urban development. Besides the excellent public transport facilities, Malad is located in close proximity to the Western Express Highway and has an important station on the Western Suburban railway line connecting it to the important social and commercial hubs of Mumbai.
Outlined by the dynamic and upcoming Mumbai Coastal Road the location is peppered by the promise of unmatched connectivity with speed, reach and convenience in and around the Metropolis of Mumbai
Decorated by the breezy and resplendent Aksa and Marve Beaches Malad is home to excellent civic amenities such as reputed hospitals land upcoming civic development projects like the coastal road extension from Malad to GB road, the demand for property in Malad will skyrocket in no time and the grand promise of great ROI.
